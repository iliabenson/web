Passed Projects

CubeSat:
A cross discipline project between CSUN students and JPL that was financed by NASA. Our mission was to build a satellite and test a JPL provided payload in low earth orbit. The name of the satellite is CSUNSAT1 and it is currently on its 3rd year of development. Aside from the radio and ground station hardware, everything was built, designed and coded by CSUN Computer Science and Engineering students. Approval for CSUNSAT2, which is a deep space mission, is currently in the planning stages.
